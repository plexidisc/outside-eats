Add to your home screen

Outside Eats works like a native app on both iOS and Android. No App Store, no download — just tap and go.

iPhone & iPad iOS

  1. Open outside-eats.com in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen
  4. Tap Add in the top right corner
  5. The Outside Eats icon will appear on your home screen

The app will open full screen without the browser address bar, just like a native app.

Android Android

  1. Open outside-eats.com in Chrome
  2. Look for the Install app banner at the bottom of the screen and tap it. If you don't see it, continue to step 3.
  3. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner of Chrome
  4. Tap Add to Home screen
  5. Tap Add to confirm

Android may show an automatic install prompt the first time you visit. Either method works.
